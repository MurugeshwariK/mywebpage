Who Are We?

Embroza isn’t just a brand — it’s a story stitched into every thread. Born from a love for slow fashion, thoughtful design, and artistic expression, we create hand-embroidered tote bags that speak your vibe.

Each bag is a canvas of creativity — carefully embroidered by local artisans, inspired by nature, dreams, and everyday magic. We believe your accessories should tell your story — soft, powerful, and uniquely you.

Our mission? To make sustainable fashion beautiful, personal, and unforgettable — one tote at a time.
